General Rule

  • The Admin and Resident Relations Officer (RRO) are the only authorized person to Add/Edit/Update Users (Owners/Tenants/Occupants) to the AzureAccess system.
  • Unit Owners/Tenants are not allowed to edit/delete information on their account. Contact Admin or RRO for any information update.
  • NO OVERLAPPING OF BOOKINGS.
  • Unit Owners/Tenants should provide valid email IDs to the Admin/RRO.
  • NO APPROVED GUEST ADVISE FORM PRESENTED BY GUEST(S), NO ID, NO ENTRY.
  • Unit Owners cannot book Guests on Siglo-managed units.

Guest Registration & Guest Arrival Rules

  • The authorized persons to book or schedule visits for guest(s) are the Unit Owners, and registered Tenants.
  • Booking requests for Guests are applicable for both “Staycation” and “Day Pass” Guests.
  • Only booked guests can enter Azure premises on the day of the arrival. Adding or changing guest(s)’ names are NOT ALLOWED.
  • Requests should be submitted at least 2-3 days prior to the visit date.
  • The limit of the number of guests per unit type should be strictly followed in the online booking which are as follows:
    • 1BR = 4 persons in total including Residents
    • 2BR = 6 persons in total including Residents
    • 3BR = 8 persons in total including Residents
  • The Unit Owners/Tenants must attach a photo/scanned copy of their Primary Visitor’s/Guest’s Passports for Foreigners and Government Issued IDs for locals.
  • All requests will be submitted to the Admin(PMO) and subject for approval.
  • Admin(PMO) will follow certain rules in approving/disapproving requests which include status in the payment of monthly dues, and others.
  • The response to the requests (either “Approved” or “Disapproved”) is expected maximum of 3 days after the submission of request.
  • Once a request is approved, the Unit Owner/Tenant and the Primary Guest will receive email notifications. The PDF file attached to the email notification sent to the Primary Guest can be printed and used as the confirmation receipt.
  • Once a request is disapproved, only the Unit Owner/Tenant will receive an email notification and he/she can file a new request for his/her Guest(s).
  • The Unit Owner/Tenant must provide an on-site Contact Person, Name and Contact Number for his/her guests to contact upon arrival and for the duration of their stay in Azure. They are also responsible in informing their guests that their presence at Azure is subject to the House Rules and Regulations of AURCC.
  • Email notification is sent to the Unit Owner/Tenant’s email once Security guard at main entrance Checked-In Guest(s) and once Checked-Out by the Tower Concierge.

SWIMMING POOL RULES:

  • Operating hours are strictly from 7am to 7pm for the Wave pool and Children's Beach while the Lap Pool is open 7am to 9pm.
  • The swimming pool is reserved for use of Azure residents and their endorsed guests through the PMO.
  • All pool users are required to register at the registration desk. Each swimmer will be issued a disposable wristband. Guest swimmers (regardless of age) exceeding the maximum number of FREE wristband per unit (1BR = 2 FREE; 2BR = 4 FREE; 3BR = 6 FREE) must pay Php 250 per person per shift per day. Strictly, NO REFUND.
  • Pool users are advised to take all necessary safety precautions. The Developer / Condominium Corp. / Property Administration Office shall not be liable for any untoward incident, accident, injury or death that may arise from the use of the swimming pool.
  • Children under age of twelve (12) must be accompanied by adults at all times. Parents/Guardians must accompany and supervise their children while in the pool / pool area and remain with them for the duration thereof. Children are not allowed to swim wearing disposable or cloth diapers.
  • Proper swimwear is required. All persons should wear clothing designed as swim wear so as to prevent damage to the swimming pool filter system.
  • All types of foods and drinks shall not be strictly prohibited in the pool and pool deck area.
  • Water sports are not allowed.
  • Expectorating or blowing of nose in the pool is strictly prohibited.
  • Persons observed by the pool attendants/ Lifeguards to  have a contagious viral or skin disease, open wounds, cold sores, inflamed eyes, or wearing bandages will not be allowed in the pool.
  • The person signing the swim waiver from must be an adult, and if children are suing the swimming pool area, the form must be signed by their parent or legal guardian. The resident/s and guest/s agree to abide by the  rules stated herein and in the House Rules of Azure Residences.
  • In any case of lightning occurrence, everyone will  be required to vacate the pool areas for your safety; Purcahsed swim bands would still ne non-refundable.
